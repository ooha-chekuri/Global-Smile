The Solution

Four modules. One trust engine.

MODULE 01
AI Treatment Visualizer
Patients upload photos and describe their concern. Gemini AI generates a plain-language "Value-Added Report" — restorative possibilities, complexity tier, consultation CTA. No diagnosis. Maximum intent.
Gemini API + Next.js
MODULE 02
Dental Tourism Calculator
Dynamic cost comparison: Vijayawada vs. New York, London, Sydney. Outputs net savings, TCDT score, and a personalized 12-hour Day 1 itinerary. Turns doubt into a data-backed decision.
Real-time · PDF Export
MODULE 03
Trust-Chain Dashboard
Live credentials, sterilization logs, anonymized patient journey milestones, post-op care coordination and teleconsultation. Every skeptic's objection — answered visually.
WhatsApp API · Realtime
MODULE 04
Local Referral Ecosystem
Secure GP-to-specialist portal with encrypted record transfer, consent management, referral status tracking, and case closure summaries. The GP stays in the loop. The patient feels continuity.
Neon DB · NextAuth

Why Now

The timing is structural, not accidental

🌐

Post-COVID Trust Deficit

International patients demand verifiable hygiene standards and transparent clinical protocols — exactly what incumbents cannot provide digitally.

🤖

AI Cost Inflection

Gemini/Claude APIs make patient-facing AI accessible at near-zero cost. The visualizer tool was impossible to build affordably 3 years ago.

✈️

Tier-2 City Moment

Vijayawada Airport's expanding international routes (Middle East, SE Asia) make first-visit logistics viable for diaspora patients for the first time.

📱

WhatsApp-First Patients

80%+ of our target diaspora segment manages healthcare inquiries via WhatsApp. We meet them there, natively, without friction.

🇮🇳

DPDP Act Compliance

India's new data protection law creates a trust moat for practices that comply publicly — most competitors have no compliance stack whatsoever.

💰

Insurance Arbitrage Window

Western dental insurance caps ($2,000/yr) are driving out-of-pocket patients to actively seek international alternatives. Search volume up 340% since 2022.
