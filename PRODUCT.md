# Product

## Register

product

## Users
* **International Patients (NRI/Diaspora):** 35–65 year olds seeking Western-standard specialist care with Indian value.
* **Local GPs:** General dentists looking for a secure, professional hand-off for complex cases.
* **Local Patients:** High-value local residents unaware of specialist prosthodontic benefits.

## Product Purpose
Transform a Tier-2 Indian specialist practice into a globally accessible "Trust Engine" that removes the opacity and anxiety of complex dental care through AI visualization and data-backed transparency.

## Brand Personality
* **Clinical:** Serious, precise, and expert.
* **Transparent:** Radical honesty about costs, protocols, and outcomes.
* **Reliable:** Safe, predictable, and elite.

## Anti-references
* Generic stock-photo dental sites with "perfect smile" models.
* Opaque "Contact for Quote" aggregators.
* Overly "salesy" or aggressive healthcare marketing.

## Design Principles
1. **Expert Precision:** Use precise medical terminology and clean, structured layouts to reflect specialist expertise.
2. **Radical Transparency:** Every figure (cost, time, protocol) must be anchored in data, not promises.
3. **Trust through Evidence:** Favor real clinical milestones and credential verification over generic marketing copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* Target WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
* Focus on readability for the 35–65 age demographic (font size, contrast).
* Support clear visual hierarchy for users in stressful medical contexts.
